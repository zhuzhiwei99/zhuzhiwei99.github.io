Research

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning, generative AI, and image processing. In pursuing my PhD, I am dedicating my efforts to 3D computer vision, including 3D reconstruction, 3D generation, 3D compression, 3D transmission, and 3D quality assessment.

Neural Mesh Refinement
Zhiwei Zhu, Xiang Gao, Lu Yu, Yiyi Liao
Frontiers of Information Technology & Electronic Engineering (FITEE), 2025

We proposes a neural mesh refinement (NMR) method that utilizes a learned geometric prior on fine shapes to adaptively refine coarse meshes through subdivision, demonstrating robust generalization.
