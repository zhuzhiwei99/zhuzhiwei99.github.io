Research

I'm interested in computer vision and deep learning. In pursuing my PhD, I am dedicating my efforts to 3D computer vision, including 3D reconstruction, 3D compression, 3D transmission, and 3D quality assessment.

VGSC: Video-based Gaussian Splat Coding
Zhiwei Zhu, Sicheng Li, Yiyi Liao, Lu Yu
2025

We propose VGSC, a Video-based Gaussian Splats Coding framework for compressing static and dynamic Gaussian splats. It features a highly modular and extensible design, and supporting both video and point cloud codecs—enabling researchers and developers to easily prototype, evaluate, and extend compression pipelines.

Neural Mesh Refinement
Zhiwei Zhu, Xiang Gao, Lu Yu, Yiyi Liao
FITEE, 2025 (Cover Article)

We proposes a neural mesh refinement (NMR) method that utilizes a learned geometric prior on fine shapes to adaptively refine coarse meshes through subdivision, demonstrating robust generalization.

Bird’s-Eye-View-Based LiDAR Point Cloud Coding For Machines
Xiang Gao, Zhiwei Zhu, Lu Yu
VCIP, 2023.

We propose an end-to-end learnt point cloud coding framework for 3D machine intelligent tasks with BEV representation, named PC4M.
